# Twofold

**See how far you've gone for each other.**

Twofold is an iOS app built specifically for long-distance couples.

Track the journeys you've taken to see each other, visualise your relationship across the world, save memories to the places they happened, and instantly create shareable snapshots of how far you've travelled for love.

## The Idea

Long-distance relationships are defined by distance — but also by all the effort two people make to close it.

Flights booked months in advance. Airport reunions. Twelve-hour layovers. Weekend trips. First dinners in new cities. Tearful goodbyes. Thousands of kilometres travelled just to spend a few days together.

Twofold turns those journeys into a visual story.

Couples can track every trip they've taken to see each other and watch their relationship history build across an interactive globe.

> **84,392 km travelled for each other.**
> That's more than twice around the Earth.

## Core Experience

### Relationship Globe

The centre of Twofold is an interactive 3D globe displaying a couple's relationship across the world.

Users can:

* View their current distance apart
* See routes between destinations
* Explore every journey they've taken to see each other
* Rotate and zoom around the globe
* Zoom into cities and locations to discover memories
* View the places that have shaped their relationship

As the relationship grows, the globe becomes a visual history of everywhere the couple has been.

### Trip Tracking

Couples can record the journeys they've taken to see each other.

Each trip can include:

* Traveller
* Origin
* Destination
* Departure and arrival dates
* Flight details
* Flight number
* Distance travelled
* Photos
* Notes and memories

Twofold calculates the distance travelled and adds the journey to the couple's shared relationship history.

Flight details may be automatically retrieved using a flight number where supported.

### Memory Map

Memories are attached to real places.

Users can save:

* Photos
* Notes
* Dates
* Locations
* Special moments

When users zoom into the globe, relationship memories appear as markers across the map.

Tapping a marker opens the memory associated with that place.

Over time, the map becomes a shared visual diary of the relationship.

### Relationship Stats

Twofold turns a couple's journey into meaningful statistics.

Stats may include:

* Total kilometres or miles travelled for each other
* Distance travelled by each partner
* Number of trips taken
* Number of flights taken
* Countries visited
* Cities visited
* Days spent together
* Days spent apart
* Longest journey
* Most visited destination

Distances can also be contextualised in more emotional and memorable ways.

> You've travelled around the Earth **2.1 times** for each other.

> You've travelled **22% of the way to the Moon** for each other.

### Next Reunion

Couples can track their next planned reunion.

The home experience may display:

* Days until the next reunion
* Who is travelling
* Destination
* Flight details
* Departure time
* Arrival time
* Distance to be travelled

The countdown creates a shared moment for both partners to look forward to.

## Shareable Journey Snapshots

Sharing is a core part of Twofold.

Users can instantly generate a visual snapshot of their relationship journey at any time.

A snapshot may include:

> **We've travelled 84,392 km for each other.**
>
> 9 trips
> 14 flights
> 4 countries
> 127 days together
>
> 🌍 That's 2.1 times around the Earth.

Snapshots can combine relationship statistics with a visual representation of the couple's routes across the globe.

Users can share snapshots directly to:

* TikTok
* Instagram Stories
* Instagram
* Messages
* Other apps through the native iOS share sheet

Snapshots should be designed as content people genuinely want to post rather than generic app-generated achievement cards.

The shared content itself acts as a discovery loop for Twofold.

**Track your journey → generate a snapshot → share your story → another long-distance couple discovers Twofold.**

## Target Audience

Twofold is designed specifically for long-distance couples.

The primary audience is:

* Gen Z and young millennial couples
* International couples
* Couples living in different cities or countries
* Couples who frequently fly or travel to see each other
* Couples who document their relationship online
* TikTok and Instagram users who share relationship content

Twofold is not intended to be a general relationship improvement app.

It does not focus on quizzes, daily relationship questions, virtual pets, or AI relationship advice.

The product is centred around **distance, journeys and shared memories**.

## Product Principles

### Distance is the Story

Distance should not just be a number displayed on a profile.

It is the central narrative of the product.

Every journey, flight and kilometre travelled contributes to the couple's story.

### The Globe is the Product

The globe is not a decorative visualisation.

It is the primary way users explore their relationship history.

Trips, routes and memories should feel spatial and connected to the places where they happened.

### Built to Be Shared

Twofold should continuously create moments worth sharing.

Relationship statistics, milestones and journeys should be easy to turn into beautiful visual content.

Users should never need to wait for an annual recap to share their journey.

### Emotional, Not Clinical

Twofold tracks data, but should never feel like a travel analytics dashboard.

Numbers should be translated into emotionally meaningful stories.

Not:

> Total distance: 84,392 km

But:

> You've crossed the Earth twice for each other.

### Private by Default

A couple's relationship history, memories and travel information are private.

Sharing is always intentional and controlled by the user.

## MVP

The initial iOS release will focus on six core experiences:

1. **Partner Linking** — invite a partner and create a shared relationship.
2. **Relationship Globe** — explore home locations, trips and relationship routes.
3. **Trip Tracking** — add journeys and record who travelled to whom.
4. **Memory Map** — attach photos and memories to real locations.
5. **Relationship Stats** — calculate distance travelled and relationship milestones.
6. **Journey Snapshots** — instantly generate and share visual summaries of the couple's journey.

The goal of the MVP is to answer one question:

> **How far have we gone for each other?**

## Platform

Twofold will launch as a native iOS application.

The initial platform focus allows Twofold to create a highly polished mobile experience with deep integration into the Apple ecosystem.

Potential iOS technologies include:

* Swift
* SwiftUI
* MapKit
* PhotosUI
* Core Location
* CloudKit or a dedicated backend service
* Native iOS share sheet

A 3D globe implementation will be evaluated based on performance, visual quality and interaction requirements.
