How it works

Set up once, together in minutes

Features

A shared home for your relationship

Relationship Globe

An interactive 3D globe showing both of you — your current distance apart, and every trip you've taken to close it.

Live Flight Tracking

Follow each other's flights in real time — status, gate, delays, and a notification the moment they land.

Memories

Save photos and moments to the exact places they happened, building a map of your shared story.

Couple Games

Bite-sized questions and games built for two, made to close the distance even when apart.

Widgets & Live Activities
