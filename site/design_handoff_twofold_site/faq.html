Getting started

Twofold is a native iOS app built for long-distance couples. It turns your relationship into a living map — track each other's flights in real time, watch the distance between you close, and save memories to the places they happened, all on a shared 3D globe.

Twofold is available on iOS today. An Android version is in the works — join the waitlist on our home page and we'll message you the moment it ships.

Once you've both installed the app, one of you sends an invite link. Tapping it pairs your accounts — from then on, every trip, flight, and memory you add belongs to both of you.
