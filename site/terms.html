Using Twofold

Twofold is a companion app for couples navigating a long-distance relationship. By using the app, you agree to provide accurate information, use it respectfully toward your partner, and not misuse features (flight tracking, memories, games, or doodles) to harass or harm another person.

You're responsible for keeping your account credentials secure. You must be old enough to form a binding agreement in your jurisdiction to use Twofold.

Subscriptions

Twofold Plus is an auto-renewing subscription billed through the App Store. You can manage or cancel it any time in your device's Settings. Either partner's active subscription unlocks Plus features for both of you.

Content you share

You retain ownership of the photos, memories, and messages you add to Twofold. By sharing content with a connected partner, you're granting them the ability to view it within the app for as long as you're connected.

Removing a partner

Either partner can end a connection at any time. Doing so archives shared data rather than deleting it immediately — either of you can permanently delete it afterward from Settings.
